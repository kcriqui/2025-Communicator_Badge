# Supercon Text Adventure

A classic text adventure with a Supercon theme!

The projector is broken in the LACM main room and your talk is in jeopardy! Can you find the missing pieces and fix the projector to save your Supercon talk??

## How to Play

- Press "Start" (F1) to begin the game.
- Use the arrow keys to scroll up/down.
- Enter your commands on the keyboard.
	- "BS" is recognized for deletion.
	- Press "Enter" to submit a command.
- Enter "help" to see other available commands.
